DDS pipeline
============

Current rule: DDS tools may write only into base Content and L10N\en.

Allowed write targets:

    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\en\...

Forbidden write targets:

    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\de\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\es\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\fr\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\it\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\ja\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\ko\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\pl\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\pt\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\ru\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\tr\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\zh-Hans\...
    UnrealEssentials\P3R\Content\L10N\zh-Hant\...

- Translators edit files in dds\.
- repack_dds.bat imports matching DDS files into base Content and L10N\en only.
- Do not mirror, copy, or name-map-rewrite DDS assets into other cultures.
- mirror_l10n_uasset.py is disabled on purpose. If it runs, something is wrong.
